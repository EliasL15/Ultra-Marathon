About the Event

We are a group of seven university students, aged 19-23, who share a deep desire to give back to our country. Despite not being financially independent, lacking established connections or influential positions, we decided to sacrifice our time and bodies to create something meaningfulâ€”a first-of-its-kind Ultra Marathon in Cyprus, all for charity.

Our mission is to give back to the community and inspire others to do the same. This event is not just about running; it's about pushing boundaries and demonstrating that with passion and dedication, we can make a difference, no matter our circumstances.

In the future, we aim to establish the toughest Ultra Marathon in the world, showcasing our island's beautiful yet ruthlessly hot and humid climate to runners from across the globe. Through these efforts, we will continue to raise funds for the intellectual and physical education of underprivileged youth in Cyprus, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to thrive.

All proceeds from this Ultra Marathon will go directly towards Red Cross Society - Youth section. Join us in making a difference and setting an example for others to follow.
